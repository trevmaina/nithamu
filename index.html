About Us

At Nithamu Investments Limited, we are dedicated to creating a just, peaceful, and sustainable world. Guided by core values such as integrity, accountability, and compassion, we champion global well-being by protecting children's rights, resolving conflicts peacefully, empowering youth, advocating for social justice, and promoting transparent governance. Join us as we work together to build a brighter future for all.

  • Learn More

Our Mission

To champion global well-being through the protection of children's rights, peaceful conflict resolution, youth empowerment in combating climate change, advocacy for social justice and promotion of transparent and inclusive governance.

Our Vision

To create a just, peaceful, and sustainable world where everyone is treated with dignity and respect, and sustainable practices are embraced.
